Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025. Short version: we collect almost nothing and sell nothing.

TL;DR — This site collects no personal data. Our apps collect no personal data. We use no trackers, no analytics, no ad networks. Read on for the full legal version.

1. Who We Are

Byte Envision is operated by Aamir Sidque, an independent Android developer based in Baramulla, Jammu & Kashmir, India. Contact: byteenvision@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

This website (GitHub Pages): No cookies. No analytics. No tracking scripts. GitHub Pages may log IP addresses in server logs as part of their infrastructure — we have no access to or control over this data.

Contact form: If you submit the contact form, your name, email and message are sent directly to our email via Formspree. We use this only to reply to you. We do not add you to any mailing list.

Our Android apps: Every Byte Envision app is built offline-first. None of our apps collect personal data, send analytics, or require account creation. See individual app privacy policies on the Play Store listing for each app.

3. Third-Party Services

This site uses no third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel). Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN — Google may log font requests. If this concerns you, the site functions normally with fonts blocked.

4. Data Retention

Emails sent via the contact form are retained in our inbox for as long as the conversation is relevant. You can request deletion at any time by emailing byteenvision@gmail.com.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we hold almost none, this is usually trivial. Email us and we'll sort it within 48 hours.

6. Changes

If we change this policy materially, we'll update the date at the top of this page. No surprises.

7. Contact

Questions? Email byteenvision@gmail.com. We read every email.
