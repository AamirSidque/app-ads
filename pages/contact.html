FAQ

Common questions

How long does an app take to build?
Simple utility apps take 4–6 weeks from kickoff to Play Store. Complex apps with backend integrations take longer. I give a realistic estimate after seeing your requirements — not an optimistic one.
Do you work remotely?
Yes, 100%. Based in Kashmir, working with clients across India and internationally. All communication via email. No unnecessary meetings.
What do you need to start?
A clear description of what the app should do, who it's for, and your deadline. The more specific, the better the quote. If you're unsure, I can help scope it.
Do you provide ongoing support?
Yes. All app projects include 30 days of post-launch support. Longer maintenance contracts available on request.
What's your payment structure?
50% upfront, 50% on delivery for new clients. Long-term clients can arrange milestone-based payments. UPI, bank transfer accepted.
Can you add ads or in-app purchases?
Yes. AdMob integration and Google Play Billing (in-app purchases / subscriptions) are available as add-ons. I can also advise on the best monetisation model for your app.
